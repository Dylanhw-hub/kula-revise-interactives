## Source 1C — FLAG

- **Image:** UDF "DON'T VOTE IN APARTHEID ELECTIONS! / FORWARD TO FREEDOM!" protest poster.
- **Cited as:** `https://www.saha.org.za/udf/don't_vote_in_apartheid_elections_forward_to_freedom.htm`
  (South African History Archive, SAHA). Accessed 25 February 2021.
- **Creator / rights holder:** UDF campaign poster, produced c.1983 (collective/anonymous
  political-movement artwork, likely originating in the Community Arts Project / UDF media
  network). Held and digitised by SAHA in its poster collection (call number AL2446).
- **Reason for flag:** Created in 1983, this is well within the South African copyright term
  (anonymous/collective works are protected for 50 years from publication, i.e. until roughly
  2033). SAHA holds and provides the digitised poster for research and education but does not
  place it under an open/Creative Commons licence that permits free reuse. No verifiable
  public-domain or open licence was found. When unsure, FLAG.
- **Rights sit with:** original UDF creators / SAHA poster collection (AL2446).
- **Used by questions:** q10 (1.3.1, 2 marks), q11 (1.3.2, 2 marks), q12 (1.4 comparison,
  4 marks) — 8 marks total. The same image is re-embedded in the `compare` source for q12.
