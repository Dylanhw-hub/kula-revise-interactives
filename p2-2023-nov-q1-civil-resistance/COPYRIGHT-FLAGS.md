- **Creator / publication:** Poster designed by COSATU media worker **Patrick Cockayne** (c.1987–1988). Reproduced in *Red on Black: The Story of the South African Poster Movement* by **Judy Seidman** (Real African Publishers / South African History Archive, 2007).
- **Reason:** A late-1980s South African political/movement poster. The artist and the compiling publisher are recent (Seidman is living; the book is a 2007/2008 publication), so the work is well within copyright term. No public-domain status and no open/Creative Commons licence found for the poster or the Seidman volume. Rights likely rest with the artist / COSATU / SAHA / the publisher.
- **Decision:** FLAG — not verifiably public domain or openly licensed.
