## Source 3D — FLAG

- **Image:** Editorial cartoon "BETWEEN A ROCK AND A HARD PLACE" — a frightened mother and
  child labelled "THE POOR" caught between a wave of coronavirus labelled "COVID-19" and a
  grim reaper robed "POVERTY & HUNGER" among graves.
- **Cited as:** appeared in an online publication of the **African News Agency (ANA)** on
  21 April 2020; reproduced via `https://jcom.sissa.it/sites/default/files/documents/JCOM_1907_2020_A08.pdf`.
  Accessed 30 May 2021.
- **Creator / rights holder:** cartoonist **H Mangena** (signed "Mangena", dated 21.04.2020),
  published by the African News Agency.
- **Reason for flag:** This is a contemporary (2020) editorial cartoon by a living, named
  artist, published by a commercial news agency. It is firmly under copyright, and editorial
  cartoons are essentially never openly licensed. No public-domain or Creative Commons licence
  exists. FLAG.
- **Rights sit with:** the cartoonist H Mangena and/or the African News Agency (ANA).
- **Used by questions:** q16 (3.4.1, 2 marks), q17 (3.4.2, 4 marks), q18 (3.5 comparison,
  4 marks) — 10 marks total. The same image is re-embedded in the `compare` source for q18.

## Notes

- The cartoon was extracted directly from the November 2021 P2 addendum, rendered at 300 DPI
